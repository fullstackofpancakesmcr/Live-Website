Full Stack of Pancakes
We are a collective of enthusiastic trainee developers based in Manchester. Our goal is to strip away all the complex metaphors, politics, and clunky communication problems you face when learning to code.

"I wouldn't call myself a full stack web developer, more like... a full stack of pancakes!"

Coding education has a communication problem, the subject matter isn't really that hard to learn, it's how it's being taught that makes it complicated - Full Stack of Pancakes are using human-centered design to demystify the skill, bringing coding to everybody.

National Coding Week is an international non-profit campaign to encourage young and old to learn and teach coding. Full Stack of Pancakes founder Sophie, is a National Coding Week Ambassador - you can read her story and what inspired her to host 7 workshops in 7 days in an interview with NCW here.
